# Recital 14

The notion of ‘biometric data’ used in this Regulation should be interpreted in light of the notion of biometric data as defined in [Article 4](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-4), point (14) of Regulation (EU) 2016/679, [Article 3](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-3), point (18) of Regulation (EU) 2018/1725 and [Article 3](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-3), point (13) of Directive (EU) 2016/680. Biometric data can allow for the authentication, identification or categorisation of natural persons and for the recognition of emotions of natural persons.

## This Recital relates to

*   [Article 3: Definitions](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-3)
