# Art. 35 AI Act - Identification numbers and lists of notified bodies

1. The Commission shall assign a single identification number to each notified body, even where a body is notified under more than one Union act.
2. **1**The Commission shall make publicly available the list of the bodies notified under this Regulation, including their identification numbers and the activities for which they have been notified. **2**The Commission shall ensure that the list is kept up to date.
