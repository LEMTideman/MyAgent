# Article 2: Scope

## Summary

The AI Act applies to anyone who places on the EU market or puts into service AI systems or places on the market general-purpose AI models, or imports or distributes AI systems in the EU. It also applies to users (deployers) of AI systems located in the EU and to product manufacturers placing on the market products with AI systems, as well as to any affected persons located in the EU.

The AI Act does not apply to natural persons using AI systems for personal non-professional activity, or any research, testing or development activity regarding AI systems or AI models. It also does not apply to matters outside the scope of EU law and does not affect Member States' competences in national security. [Article 2](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-2) also excludes certain other AI systems from the scope of the AI Act.

The summaries are meant to provide helpful explanation but are not legal binding.
