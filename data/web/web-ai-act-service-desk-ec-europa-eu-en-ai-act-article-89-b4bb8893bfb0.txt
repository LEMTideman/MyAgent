# Article 89: Monitoring actions

## Chapter IX: Post-Market Monitoring, Information Sharing and Market Surveillance
### Section 5: Supervision, Investigation, Enforcement and Monitoring in Respect of Providers of General-Purpose AI Models

### Summary
This article outlines the AI Office's role in monitoring the implementation and compliance of providers of general-purpose AI models with the regulation. Downstream providers can lodge complaints alleging infringements, which must include specific details about the provider, the relevant facts, the provisions concerned, and any additional relevant information.

The summaries are meant to provide helpful explanation but are not legal binding.
