# Article 104: Amendment to Regulation (EU) No 168/2013

In [Article 22(5)](/en/ai-act/article-22) of Regulation (EU) No 168/2013, the following subparagraph is added:

"When adopting delegated acts pursuant to the first subparagraph concerning Artificial Intelligence systems which are safety components within the meaning of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 of the European Parliament and of the Council[\*](#ntr*-L_202401689EN.000101-E0060), the requirements set out in Chapter III, Section 2, of that Regulation shall be taken into account."

## Footnotes

\* When adopting delegated acts pursuant to the first subparagraph concerning Artificial Intelligence systems which are safety components within the meaning of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 of the European Parliament and of the Council.
