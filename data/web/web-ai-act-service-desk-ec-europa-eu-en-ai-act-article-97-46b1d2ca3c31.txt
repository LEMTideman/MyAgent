# Article 97: Exercise of the delegation

## Chapter XI: Delegation of Power and Committee Procedure

### Summary

This article outlines the delegation of power to the Commission to adopt delegated acts related to specific articles of the regulation. This power is conferred for a period of five years from August 1, 2024, and can be extended or revoked by the European Parliament or the Council. Before adopting a delegated act, the Commission must consult experts designated by each Member State and notify the European Parliament and the Council. The delegated acts will enter into force unless there is an objection from either the European Parliament or the Council within a specified period.

The summaries are meant to provide helpful explanation but are not legal binding.
