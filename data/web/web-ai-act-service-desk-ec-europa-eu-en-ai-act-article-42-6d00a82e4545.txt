# Article 42: Presumption of conformity with certain requirements

## Chapter III: High-Risk AI Systems

### Section 5: Standards, Conformity Assessment, Certificates, Registration

#### Summary
High-risk AI systems trained and tested on data relevant to their intended use are presumed to comply with the requirements of [Article 10(4)](/en/ai-act/article-10) on datasets. Additionally, systems certified under a cybersecurity scheme pursuant to Regulation (EU) 2019/881 are presumed to comply with the cybersecurity requirements of [Article 15](/en/ai-act/article-15), to the extent covered by the certification.

The summaries are meant to provide helpful explanation but are not legal binding.
