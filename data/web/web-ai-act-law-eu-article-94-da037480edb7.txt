Art. 94 AI Act - Procedural rights of economic operators of the general-purpose AI model
======================================================================================

[Article 18 of Regulation (EU) 2019/1020](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=CELEX:32019R1020#d1e2009-1-1) shall apply mutatis mutandis to the providers of the general-purpose AI model, without prejudice to more specific procedural rights provided for in this Regulation.

Suitable Recitals
-----------------

* [163] Support for the AI Office by scientific panel
* [164] Competencies of the AI Office
