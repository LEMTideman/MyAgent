# Article 107: Amendment to Regulation (EU) 2018/858

In [Article 5](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-5) of Regulation (EU) 2018/858 the following paragraph is added:

> "4. When adopting delegated acts pursuant to paragraph 3 concerning Artificial Intelligence systems which are safety components within the meaning of Regulation (EU) 2024/1689 of the European Parliament and of the Council[\*](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-107#ntr*-L_202401689EN.000101-E0063), the requirements set out in Chapter III, Section 2, of that Regulation shall be taken into account."
