# Recital 100

## When a general-purpose AI model is integrated into or forms part of an AI system, this system should be considered to be a general-purpose AI system when, due to this integration, this system has the capability to serve a variety of purposes. A general-purpose AI system can be used directly, or it may be integrated into other AI systems.

### This Recital relates to

*   [Article 3: Definitions](/en/ai-act/article-3)
*   [Article 53: Obligations for providers of general-purpose AI models](/en/ai-act/article-53)
