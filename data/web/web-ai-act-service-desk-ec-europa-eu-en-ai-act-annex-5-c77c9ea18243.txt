# Annex V

## Overview

The EU declaration of conformity referred to in [Article 47](https://ai-act-service-desk.ec.europa.eu/en/ai-act/article-47), shall contain all of the following information:

1. **AI system name and type**: A statement that the AI system is identified and traceable.
2. **Provider details**: The name and address of the provider or, where applicable, its authorised representative.
3. **Issuing responsibility**: A statement that the EU declaration of conformity is issued solely by the provider.
4. **Conformity with regulations**: A statement that the AI system complies with the EU Act and any other relevant Union laws providing for the issuing of the EU declaration of conformity.
5. **Processing of personal data**: Where applicable, a statement that the AI system complies with GDPR and EU Directive (EU) 2016/679.
6. **Standards and specifications**: References to any harmonised standards or common specifications used in relation to which conformity is declared.
7. **Notified body information**: If applicable, a description of the conformity assessment procedure performed by a notified body, including identification of the certificate issued.
8. **Date and authorship**: The place and date of issue, as well as indications about who signed it on behalf of whom.

View the [official text](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=OJ:L_202401689). The text used in this tool is the 'Artificial Intelligence Act (Regulation (EU) 2024/1689), Official version of 13 June 2024'.
