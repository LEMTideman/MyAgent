# Recital 152

In order to support adequate enforcement as regards AI systems and reinforce the capacities of the Member States, Union AI testing support structures should be established and made available to the Member States.

### This Recital relates to

*   [Article 84: Union AI testing support structures](/en/ai-act/article-84)

### Section 3: Obligations of Providers and Deployers of High-Risk AI Systems and Other Parties

#### Article 16:
[Link to article]  
**Human Rights Impact Assessment:**  
For high-risk AI systems, human rights impact assessments must be conducted.  
**Risk Management System:**  
A risk management system must be implemented.  
**Data and Data Governance:**  
Provision of data must be ensured.  
**Technical Documentation:**  
Documentation must be kept.  
**Record Keeping:**  
Records must be kept.  
**Transparency and Provision of Information:**  
Information about the use of artificial intelligence must be provided.  
**Human Oversight:**  
Human oversight mechanisms must be established.  
**Accuracy, Robustness, and Cybersecurity:**  
Ensuring accuracy, robustness, and security is necessary.
