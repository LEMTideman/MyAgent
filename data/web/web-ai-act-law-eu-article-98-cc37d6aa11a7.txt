## Art. 98 AI Act Committee procedure

1. The Commission shall be assisted by a committee. That committee shall be a committee within the meaning of [Regulation (EU) No 182/2011](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=CELEX:32011R0182).
2. Where reference is made to this paragraph, [Article 5 of Regulation (EU) No 182/2011](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=CELEX:32011R0182#d1e387-13-1) shall apply.
