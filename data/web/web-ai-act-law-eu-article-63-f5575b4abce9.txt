# Art. 63 AI Act - Derogations for specific operators

## Main Content

1. Microenterprises within the meaning of [Recommendation 2003/361/EC](https://eur-lex.europa.eu/legal-content/EN/TXT/HTML/?uri=CELEX:32003H0361) may comply with certain elements of the quality management system required by [Article 17](https://ai-act-law.eu/article/17/) of this Regulation in a simplified manner, provided that they do not have partner enterprises or linked enterprises within the meaning of that Recommendation.
2. For that purpose, the Commission shall develop guidelines on the elements of the quality management system which may be complied with in a simplified manner considering the needs of microenterprises, without affecting the level of protection or the need for compliance with the requirements in respect of high-risk AI systems.
