# Article 111: AI systems already placed on the market or put into service and general-purpose AI models already placed on the market

## Summary

AI systems that are part of large-scale IT systems established by specific legal acts listed in [Annex X](/en/ai-act/annex-10) and have been placed on the market or put into service before August 2, 2027, must comply with the AI Act by December 31, 2030. Operators of other high-risk AI systems placed on the market or put in service before August 2, 2026, must comply with the AI Act if they undergo significant design changes after August 2, 2026. In any case, providers and deployers of high-risk AI systems intending to be used by public authorities shall comply with the AI Act by 2 August 2030. Providers of general-purpose AI models that have been placed on the market before August 2, 2025, must comply with the AI Act by August 2, 2027.

The summaries are meant to provide helpful explanation but are not legal binding.

### Chapter XIII: Final Provisions

#### Article 111: AI systems already placed on the market or put into service and general-purpose AI models already placed on the market

##### Summary

AI systems that are components of the large-scale IT systems established by the legal acts listed in [Annex X](/en/ai-act/annex-10) that have been placed on the market or put into service before 2 August 2027 shall be brought into compliance with this Regulation by 31 December 2030.

The requirements laid down in this Regulation shall be taken into account in the evaluation of each large-scale IT system established by the legal acts listed in [Annex X](/en/ai-act/annex-10) to be undertaken as provided for in those legal acts and where those legal acts are replaced or amended.

##### Article 111.0. Without prejudice to the application of [Article 5](/en/ai-act/article-5) as referred to in [Article 113(3)](/en/ai-act/article-113), point (a), AI systems which are components of the large-scale IT systems established by the legal acts listed in [Annex X](/en/ai-act/annex-10) that have been placed on the market or put into service before 2 August 2027 shall be brought into compliance with this Regulation by 31 December 2030.

The requirements laid down in this Regulation shall be taken into account in the evaluation of each large-scale IT system established by the legal acts listed in [Annex X](/en/ai-act/annex-10) to be undertaken as provided for in those legal acts and where those legal acts are replaced or amended.

##### Article 111.Ⅰ Without prejudice to the application of [Article 5](/en/ai-act/article-5) as referred to in [Article 113(3)](/en/ai-act/article-113), point (a), this Regulation shall apply to operators of high-risk AI systems, other than the systems referred to in paragraph I of this Article, that have been placed on the market or put into service before 2 August II, only if, as from that date, those systems are subject to significant changes in their designs. In any case, the providers and deployers of high-risk AI systems intended to be used by public authorities shall take the necessary steps to comply with the requirements and obligations of this Regulation by II August III.
