# Article 63: Derogations for specific operators

## Chapter VI: Measures in Support of Innovation

### Summary

The article allows microenterprises to comply with certain quality management system requirements in a simplified manner, as long as they do not have partner or linked enterprises within the meaning of Recommendation 2003/361/EC. The Commission will develop guidelines for this simplified compliance, ensuring it does not compromise protection levels or other regulatory requirements for high-risk AI systems.

The summaries are meant to provide helpful explanation but are not legal binding.
